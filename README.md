# Jugaad Junction

Welcome to Jugaad Junction. It is basically a survival toolkit for students masquerading as a modern web application. Have you ever urgently needed an A4 sheet while writing an assignment during class or realized you forgot your powerbank right when you started watching reels in the second bench? We have all been there. This platform weaponizes your desperation into a structured economy.

## What is it exactly?

It is a hyper local peer to peer resource exchange platform. But let us be honest, it is an organized black market for class notes, attendance proxy requests and spare stationery. 

Instead of relying on random WhatsApp groups where your request gets ignored, you post a ticket here. People fulfill your ticket, they get Social Credit, and you get your A4 sheet.

## Features

* **Ticketing System**: Need something? Create a request ticket. Have something? Post an offer. Watch people bargain over a half used notebook like it is real estate.
* **Social Credit System**: Gain points for helping others. Ascend from the rank of an absolute nobody to a campus legend. It is like a video game, but the graphics are just your actual classmates.
* **Negotiations**: Because simply accepting a price is no fun. You can haggle over the price of a borrowed pen directly in the user interface. 
* **Admin Oversight**: Someone acting up? Do not worry, Roll Number 9999 is watching. The admin panel lets the overlords resolve complaints and manipulate credit scores.
